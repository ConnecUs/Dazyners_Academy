Frequently Asked Questions

How do I prepare for certification?

Complete the corresponding learning path, practice with hands-on projects, and review the certification preparation guide provided in each course.

What is the certification process?

The certification includes a theoretical assessment, practical coding challenges, and a final project submission. You must score 80% or higher to pass.

How long does certification take?

The certification exam takes 3-4 hours. Most candidates complete their preparation within 2-3 months while working through the learning path.

What happens if I don't pass?

You can retake the certification after 14 days. We provide detailed feedback and areas for improvement after each attempt.
